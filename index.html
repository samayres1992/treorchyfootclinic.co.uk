Biography

Debra Ayres is a qualified podiatrist holding a Bachelor of Science with honours (BSc hons) degree, and has been a member of the Royal College of Podiatry for over 14 years. A Podiatrist is a protected title with the hcpc therefore ensuring the practice is fully insured and regulated. Treorchy Foot Clinic has now been open on the high street for ten years.
